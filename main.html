Kerala


Kerala is most beautiful place in India. It can be considered as heaven of India. It have mountains, forests, beaches and backwaters. We can enjoy the true nature here. The architectures of houses are different and beautiful at the same time. Kerala is also famous for it's hospitality and luxury hotels/resorts. Kerala offer all types of resorts, including couple to family resorts. This place is suitable for having honeymoons or for enjoying best time with your family. The real beauty of nature and culture exists here.

Best Attractions

  1. Wayanad(famous for hospitality and adventurous activities)
  2. Alleppey(Most famous for houseboat stays and back water ride)
  3. Munnar(Tea plantation)
  4. Thiruvananthapuram(Temples and culture)
  5. Kumarkom(Best beaches and houseboat stays)

Must try Foods

  • South indian cuisine(including idli-sambhar, wada, dosa, etc)
  • Nariyal Paani
  • Nadan Kozhi Varuthathu(Spicy Chicken Fry)
  • Prawn Curry
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
  • Adda Pradhaman(Sweets)
